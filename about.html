About Me

I am Arghavan Dalvand. At the age of 18, I began my Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology (IT) at the Islamic Azad University of Qazvin. During this time, I entered the MRL Small-Size Robots Laboratory as a member of the software team.

Throughout my six-year tenure with the MRL team, I participated in several international and Asian competitions and won three international titles. After completing my undergraduate studies, I moved to Italy to pursue a Master’s degree in Robotics Engineering at the University of Genoa.

To complete my thesis project, I spent 15 months research at the Italian Institute of Technology (IIT). I worked on Bluetooth communication to integrate Comeback Mobility smart crutches into the TwinMed exoskeleton environment, a system developed by IIT for individuals with spinal cord injury (SCI). This integration enabled real-time data streaming from the crutches, allowing for continuous monitoring and assessment of the user’s stability during movement.
