About Me

I am Arghavan Dalvand. At the age of 18, I began my Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology (IT) at the Islamic Azad University of Qazvin. During this time, I entered the MRL Small-Size Robots Laboratory as a member of the software team.

Throughout my six-year tenure with the MRL team, I participated in several international and Asian competitions and won three international titles. After completing my undergraduate studies, I moved to Italy to pursue a Master’s degree in Robotics Engineering at the University of Genoa. To complete my thesis project, I spent 15 months conducting research at the Italian Institute of Technology (IIT).
